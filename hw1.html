Biography

Linnus Torvalds is Finnish-American software engineer who is the creator and principal developer of Linux kernal,which is the kernal for a number oprating system. He also created the version control system Git. Torvalds was born in december 28 ,1969 in Helsinki,Finland. He studies Computer Science at university of Helsinki,he developed the linux kernal as a student project in 1991. Since then, the Linux kernal become the most widely used kernals in the world,powering everything from smartphones to supercomputers.

  • 2014 - Inducted into the Hall of fame of the computer history museum in 2014.
  • 2015 - Received the IEEE Computer Society Computer Pioneer Award.
  • 2019 - Received the 2019 ACM SIGOPS HAll of Fame Award.
